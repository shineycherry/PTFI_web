About Us

At PTFI, we specialize in developing cutting-edge privacy technologies designed for financial intelligence. Our mission is to create secure, innovative solutions that enable safe data sharing and analysis in the financial sector. We are committed to helping financial institutions protect sensitive information while harnessing the power of advanced analytics.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leader in privacy-enhancing technologies, offering solutions that drive financial intelligence while ensuring data privacy and security. We aim to empower financial institutions to operate with greater transparency, efficiency, and trust.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop privacy-preserving technologies that enable secure data sharing and collaboration, providing financial institutions with the tools they need to detect fraud, comply with regulations, and enhance decision-making processes.

What We Do

  • Develop privacy-enhancing technologies such as Homomorphic Encryption, Differential Privacy, and Federated Learning.
  • Enable secure data sharing and analysis across financial institutions.
  • Provide solutions for fraud detection, compliance, and financial intelligence.
  • Collaborate with industry experts to stay ahead of emerging privacy challenges.

Our Values

At PTFI, we are driven by the following core values:

  • Innovation: We continuously explore new technologies and ideas to provide innovative privacy solutions.
  • Security: We prioritize data security and privacy in all our solutions.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of collaboration, working together with our clients and partners to create impactful solutions.
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty, transparency, and a commitment to delivering high-quality solutions.
